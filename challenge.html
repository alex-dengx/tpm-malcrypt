Instructions:

The challenges are simple and hopefully educational. You'll at least need Wireshark to play. Since the talk is late in the conference schedule each challenge is designed to take no more than 20 minutes to solve.

Easy-Mode:

The first correct answer will receive a CryptoCape! The next 3 correct answers will receive an epic commemerative DC22 CHUCKWAGON!
